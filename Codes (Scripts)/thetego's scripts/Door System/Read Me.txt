Setup instructions:


*Attach CameraHit.cs to your camera (You can assign your crosshair to Cursor (Image))

*Attach DoorSystem.cs to your door gameobject (if it has a parent as pivot,attach to parent 
but you should add collider to parent. Otherwise you need to attach to directly mesh object. And the last thing
you need to add a tag called "Door" to the gameobject which has the script.

*Door open/close key is "E".

*The Angle variable in the DoorSystem.cs should be adjusted according to rotation values of door object.
